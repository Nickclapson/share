Challenges & Opportunities

Conversion Rate Decline

Conversions fell 34.5% from 116 in October 2024 to 76 in September 2025. Improved engagement metrics suggest content relevance is strong, but conversion optimisation on key landing pages requires attention. This represents a significant opportunity to capitalise on existing ranking improvements.

Traffic-to-Ranking Mismatch

The 28% traffic decline despite ranking improvements suggests either:

  • Tracked keywords are predominantly lower-volume terms
  • Click-through rates need optimisation through title and meta description improvements
  • Seasonal business cycles are affecting search demand
Recent Traffic Volatility

Traffic declined 9.6% from July to September 2025, suggesting either seasonal factors or early signs of algorithm adjustments. Continued monitoring and tactical content adjustments will be required.

Strategic Recommendations

1
Optimise for High-Volume Keywords
Expand keyword targeting to include higher search volume terms to convert ranking improvements into increased traffic. Focus on commercial intent keywords with measurable search volume and clear business value.
2
Improve Click-Through Rate
Audit and optimise title tags and meta descriptions across top-ranking pages to improve CTR. Test different messaging approaches to appeal to search intent whilst maintaining keyword relevance and improving visibility in SERPs.
3
Conversion Rate Optimisation
Implement conversion optimisation across landing pages. With engagement metrics improving significantly, focus on clearer calls-to-action, streamlined forms, product recommendations, and trust signals to convert engaged visitors into customers.
4
Monitor Seasonal Trends
Analyse search volume fluctuations by season and product category. Adjust content strategy and allocate paid search support during high-demand periods to maximise revenue and offset organic search seasonality.
